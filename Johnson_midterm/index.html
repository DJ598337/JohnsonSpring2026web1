🍓 Sparkle

Using the same movement of the lava lamp I created a empty void of flowers that are just spining, glowing and floating within a space of sparkle. I made this animation because I wanted an excuse to use emoji's as well as glitter. I love how tranquil it is. It's something you would put on if you want to meditate or just have as a screen saver. One thing I would like to know and change in the future is how to make it so the location and the spinning/ floating is different every time you click on the image. Also how to add music. A lofi track or just the waves would be a great addition to this piece.

Lava_Lamp

I tried to make a lava lamp artwork using animation and i really like it. So I made the "glass" of the lava lamp and put different colored circles within it and had them in different locations and speeds within the glass so it looks like its moving naturally within the lamp. I didn't put much thought in the colors but more of the "how" process which I found really difficult. When I started the circles were out of the lamp, then the circles were going too fast or were bunched together. Im glad I figured it out, but unfortunately it wasn't the exact vision. It was supposed to be three lamps with different shapes and colors in them, but I just didn't have the brain cells to figure out how to duplicate my work but on the left and right hand side of the first lamp. To compensate I put some words of wisdom which I think you (yes YOU) will find funny.

Hidden_art 🍓

This animation is a hidden art animation where, when hovered over, the image disappears to reveal another image. I chose the second image to be the Kermit dancing gif because I love kermit and I think its funny to believe that the art would be something meaningful or serious but instead it's a well known gif that would make you laugh. I like how it turned out but one problem I ran into was trying to add confetti to the hidden image so that when you reveal the gif the confetti comes out. Sadly the confetti when I tried it was continuous or in intervals, which wasn't what I wanted.
